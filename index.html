Band Bio

Sinclair Vernon met guitarist Riley Miller through a mutual friend in late 2010, and they began to translate the songs she had started on keys into the hard, 90s-influenced rock that they had both grown up on and which was in short supply in the alt-folk and bluegrass-heavy area of Carrboro, NC. They later secured bassist Keith Lewis and drummer Tim Wall. The band’s first full-length album, End of the Universe, was recorded at Fidelitorium Recording Studios and produced by Ted Comerford. The album was released in January 2013 and preceded by the first single, Road Rising.
